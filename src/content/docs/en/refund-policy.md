# Refund Policy – CubeOcean Cloud

## 1. General Terms
This Refund Policy governs refund requests for services provided by **CubeOcean Cloud**. By making a purchase, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

## 2. Refund Eligibility
Refunds may only be granted under the following conditions:
- Double payment occurred
- Service is inaccessible due to a system error from CubeOcean Cloud
- The service significantly differs from its described functionality
- A verifiable transaction error has occurred

## 3. Non-Refundable Cases
Refunds will not be issued if:
- The service has been fully or partially used
- The refund request is submitted after the service period has ended
- Issues are caused by user misconfiguration or user error
- Violation of CubeOcean Cloud Terms of Service
- Change of mind after purchase

## 4. Refund Request Time Limit
Refund requests must be submitted within:
- **7 calendar days** from the date of successful payment

Requests submitted after this period will not be accepted.

## 5. Refund Request Procedure
To request a refund, users must contact official support and provide:
- Transaction ID / invoice
- Proof of payment
- Detailed explanation of the refund reason
- Account or service information related to the request

## 6. Verification Process
Each request will be reviewed by the CubeOcean Cloud team. We reserve the right to approve or reject any refund request based on eligibility.

## 7. Processing Time
If approved, refunds will be processed within:
- **3 – 14 business days**, depending on the payment method used

## 8. Refund Method
Refunds will be issued using the original payment method whenever possible. Exceptions may apply due to payment gateway limitations.

## 9. Subscription Services
- All subscription services are **non-refundable once activated or renewed**
- Canceling a service does not automatically trigger a refund
- Users are responsible for canceling renewal before the next billing cycle

## 10. Policy Changes
CubeOcean Cloud reserves the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time without prior notice. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly.
