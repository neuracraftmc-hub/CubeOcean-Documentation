## 1. Introduction

### Our Vision

We built CubeOcean to support the ecosystem of small to medium developers so they can build, test, and run their systems with high performance at an affordable cost. We believe technology should be accessible to everyone, not only large companies.

CubeOcean is currently developed as a community-driven technology project focused on innovation and collaboration, and it is still in an early development stage.

### Why Join Us?

* A collaborative environment focused on experimentation and learning
* Opportunity to grow alongside a continuously evolving project
* Flexible and open to new ideas
* Real impact on the developer community

## 3. Position Details

### Admin

**Job Description:**

* Manage daily operational data and platform activities
* Assist in system and user monitoring
* Handle basic reports and issue tracking

**Requirements:**

* Detail-oriented and responsible
* Basic understanding of hosting panels (preferred)
* Good communication skills

---

### Sales

**Job Description:**

* Promote CubeOcean hosting services
* Find and manage potential customers
* Build relationships with users and communities

**Requirements:**

* Strong communication and confidence
* Basic understanding of hosting products
* Active in communities (preferred)
