# Privacy Policy – CubeOcean Cloud

## 1. Introduction

CubeOcean Cloud ("we", "our", or "the platform") is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use CubeOcean Cloud services.

By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### a. Account Information

* Username
* Email address
* Login credentials (hashed password)
* Other account information provided during registration

### b. Technical Data

* IP address
* Device type and browser
* Operating system
* Usage logs and system activity

### c. Usage Data

* Dashboard activity
* Cloud feature usage
* API access (if applicable)

### d. Payment Data (if applicable)

* Transaction status
* Payment method information (processed by third-party providers; we do not store full card details)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data for the following purposes:

* To provide and operate CubeOcean Cloud services
* To maintain and improve system performance and user experience
* To ensure security and prevent abuse or fraud
* To provide customer support
* To send important service-related notifications
* For internal analytics and feature development
* To develop, train, and improve artificial intelligence (AI) systems, including chatbot models and machine learning algorithms based on user interactions and activity data

User activity data may be processed to improve response quality, system accuracy, and AI-based features within the platform.

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data, including:

* Data encryption
* Firewalls and server protection
* Monitoring for suspicious activity
* Restricted internal access controls

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal user data.

However, data may be shared in the following cases:

* With third-party service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processors)
* When required by law or legal authorities
* To protect against abuse, fraud, or security threats

All third parties are required to maintain confidentiality and data protection standards.

---

## 6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

* Access their personal data
* Update or correct their information
* Request account deletion
* Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)

Requests can be submitted through official CubeOcean Cloud support channels.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain data as long as the account remains active or as necessary to provide services.

Data may be deleted if:

* The user deletes their account
* The account remains inactive for an extended period
* Required for legal compliance

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

CubeOcean Cloud is not intended for users below the legal age required in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

CubeOcean Cloud may update this Privacy Policy at any time.

Changes will be announced through the platform or official communication channels. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
