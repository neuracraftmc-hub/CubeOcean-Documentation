# Cookie Policy – CubeOcean Cloud

**Platform:** CubeOcean Cloud

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how CubeOcean Cloud uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze platform usage, and support service development.

By using CubeOcean Cloud services, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

---

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies help websites recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or activity.

---

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the platform to function properly. Without them, core features such as authentication and security cannot work.

Examples include:

* User login sessions
* Security authentication
* Basic navigation functions

---

### 3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the platform to remember your preferences to improve your experience.

Examples include:

* Selected language
* UI settings
* Interface preferences

---

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the platform.

Data collected may include:

* Pages visited
* Time spent on the platform
* Feature usage and interactions

This data is used for improving services and platform development.

---

### 3.4 Performance and Debugging Cookies

These cookies help detect errors, improve performance, and ensure system stability.

---

## 4. Use of Cookie Data

Data collected through cookies may be used for:

* Improving service quality
* Developing new features
* Analyzing system performance
* Training AI-based chatbot or machine learning models (in anonymized form)

---

## 5. Third-Party Cookies

CubeOcean Cloud may use third-party services (such as analytics or security providers) that may place cookies on your device. We do not directly control these third-party cookies.

---

## 6. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

Common options include:

* Deleting cookies in browser settings
* Rejecting cookies when first visiting the site
* Adjusting privacy settings in your browser

---

## 7. Cookie Storage

Cookies may be:

* **Session Cookies:** Deleted when the browser is closed
* **Persistent Cookies:** Stored for a defined period or until manually deleted

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

CubeOcean Cloud may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Updates will be announced through the platform or official pages.

---
